Make Your Joint Divorce* Process Manageable

Forms, deadlines, tasks... Divorce can be a difficult enough time as it is without having to navigate through confusing legal language. That's why this online tool is here: to help you get through the details, procedures, and paperwork.

How it Works

1 Answer Questions

Our completely free and secure online questionnaire will save your work so you can proceed at your own pace.

2 Print Your Forms

Once you're done with the questions, you'll print your papers and file at your local court registry. That’s all there is to it!

*Beta Service - Joint Divorce Without Children Only

For now, this service only handles the creation of paperwork for divorces where both partners agree they want a divorce (Joint Divorce) and DO NOT have any dependent children. In the future this service will be expanded to help those WITH children.
